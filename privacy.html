Privacy Policy

Your trust is our priority. Learn how Buylix collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

📅 Effective date: June 12, 2026 | Last revised: June 12, 2026

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

At Buylix ("we," "us," "our"), we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your information when you visit our website, use our mobile app, or interact with our services (collectively, the "Services"). By using Buylix, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

🔒 Our Promise: We never sell your personal data to third parties. Any sharing of data is solely for operational purposes and with your consent where required.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The types of information we collect include:

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, shipping/billing address, and password when you create an account.
  • Order & Payment Details: Purchase history, payment method information (processed securely by our PCI-compliant partners).
  • Communications: Any information you provide when contacting customer support, leaving reviews, or participating in surveys/promotions.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device & Usage Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages visited, time spent, and clickstream data.
  • Location Data: Approximate geolocation based on IP address (you can disable location permissions in your browser).
  • Cookies & Tracking Technologies: We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to enhance user experience, analyze trends, and administer the website.

3. How We Use Your Information

Buylix uses your personal data for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Order Processing & Fulfillment: To process payments, ship products, provide order updates, and handle returns.
  • Customer Support: To respond to inquiries, resolve disputes, and provide technical assistance.
  • Personalization: To recommend products, show tailored content, and remember your preferences.
  • Marketing & Promotions: With your consent, to send newsletters, special offers, and event notifications (you can unsubscribe anytime).
  • Legal & Security: To detect fraud, prevent abuse, comply with legal obligations, and enforce our Terms of Service.

4. Sharing & Disclosure of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. However, we may share your data in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who assist with payment processing, shipping, email delivery, analytics, and marketing (they are contractually obligated to protect your data).
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, subpoena, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Buylix, our users, or others.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred (you will be notified via email or prominent notice).
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share your data for a specific purpose.
📦 Shipping & Logistics: To deliver your orders, we share your name, address, and contact details with carriers like FedEx, USPS, DHL, and local postal services.

5. Your Privacy Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access & Portability: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data, subject to legal or contractual exceptions.
  • Opt-Out of Marketing: Click "unsubscribe" in any promotional email or adjust notification settings in your account.
  • Do Not Track Signals: Our website currently does not respond to "Do Not Track" browser signals, but you may adjust cookie preferences via our cookie banner.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@buylix.com. We will respond within 30 days. For EU/UK residents, we comply with GDPR; for California residents, see Section 9 (CCPA Notice).

6. Data Security & Retention

We implement industry-standard security measures, including SSL encryption, firewalls, and restricted access controls, to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Typically, account data is retained for 7 years after last activity, unless you request deletion.

7. International Data Transfers

Buylix is based in the United States. If you access our Services from outside the US, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the US where our servers and central database are located. By using Buylix, you consent to this transfer. We ensure adequate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) for data transfers from the EEA, UK, or Switzerland.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete such information.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with additional rights, including the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion, opt-out of the "sale" of personal information (we do not sell your data), and non-discrimination for exercising your rights. To submit a CCPA request, email privacy@buylix.com or call our toll-free number +1 (833) 284-9529. Authorized agents may submit requests on your behalf with proper documentation.

🌐 Do Not Sell My Info: Buylix does not sell your personal information for monetary or valuable consideration. For more details, see our "Notice at Collection."

10. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance functionality, analyze site traffic, and personalize content. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings or our cookie consent tool. Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are necessary for core operations (e.g., shopping cart). Third-party analytics (e.g., Google Analytics) help us understand user behavior; you can install the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The "Last revised" date at the top indicates when the policy was last updated. Material changes will be notified via email (if you have an account) or a prominent notice on our website before the changes become effective. Your continued use of Buylix after any updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

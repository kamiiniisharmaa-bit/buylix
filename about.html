How Buylix began

Founded in 2022 by two friends who grew tired of fast fashion and opaque e-commerce, Buylix started as a tiny pop-up in Brooklyn. What began as a curated collection of 15 handmade bags quickly expanded into a global platform for conscious shoppers who refuse to compromise on style or values.

Today, Buylix partners with over 80 ethical brands and independent designers across 12 countries — all while maintaining our original promise: exceptional products, transparent sourcing, and customer delight. Our name "Buylix" represents the fusion of "buy" + "lyx" (luxury redefined for the modern world).
